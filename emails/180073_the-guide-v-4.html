(( MIX OF THE WEEK ))
((07 | 19 | 15)) R U R A L , J A P A N // The Long Count Cycle // Don't miss The Long Count Cycle at the Twice as Proper 9 Year Anniversary this Saturday! Their mind-blowing set from Rural Festival last summer was posted last month, so let's just call this the mix of the year.

Sign up to receive our weekly emails: technoqueers.com

See you on the dance floor! <3 The Techno Queers
Thursday
Purple Trax Label Night VOL X: Brian DeGraw / Yves Tumor / Riobamba / Trust Image // These kids love to get FREAKY. And it’s Bossa, so the threshold for that is already pretty low. Come for the eclectic, experimental sounds, stay for the jalapeno tequila.
Friday
Mysteries IV with Panabrite, Further Reductions, William Selman, Patrick Russell & Ken Meier // Mysteries of the Deep started as a podcast for ambient junkies and has grown into a gorgeously-executed event. Cinematic visuals are carefully layered throughout the room to enhance the vibe. Pop a tab, take a seat, go on a cosmic journey with kindred souls. BYO pillow.
Inhalants, Ciarra Black, m.cro, and well being at Silent Barn // Stop by this gritty DIY space for some equally jagged techno -- harsh, heavy, and perfect for Friday the 13th.
Saturday
Technopolis - Red Bull Music Academy Festival New York // We just splooged looking at this lineup. It’s a who’s who of rising techno stars from the Brooklyn underground scene- Volvox, Umfang, Aurora Halal, Ital, Via App, Bookworms, Max McFerren, & Dust, + house grooves by Anthony Naples and Galcher Lustwerk thrown in for good measure. Bless the souls of Red Bull Music Academy for putting this night together (but we’ll still be drinking Club Mate at the pregame, #sorrynotsorry.)
Twice as Proper - 9 Year Anniversary w/ Dino Sabatini (USA Debut) // Twice as Proper has been throwing underground techno parties for almost a decade, at one of the best lofts in Brooklyn. Wanna experience a techno party that’s totally untouched by club kids? This is it. They go very late, so this is a good afters option.
Still thirsty?
FRIDAY // Sublimate: Jane Fitz, Jenifa Mayanja & Dakini9 // It’s ladies' night at Sublimate! These three talented warriors of underground sound will be throwing down deep house cuts and touches of acid until well past sunrise.
SATURDAY // 808 | Derrick Carter, Manchildblack, Turtle Bugg // Rooftop. Fried Chicken. Impeccable music from house legends old and new. Yeah, it’s at Output and yes we hate clubs, but their rooftop parties tend to be better as vibes go. And did we mention fried chicken? Check it out.
