The FDA Just Expanded Access to MDMA as a Therapy for PTSD // 10 sites across the nation will receive approval to administer the drug under a doctor’s supervision. Harvard researchers find microbial toxin in Juul pods // 46 percent of the 54 Juul pods they analyzed contained detectable traces of glucan. Glucan is a component of fungal cell walls that can cause inflammation in the airways and lead to long-term lung damage.

A previous study conducted by largely the same team of researchers published in April found that 17 of 75 e-cigarette products from 10 top selling U.S. brands were also contaminated with endotoxin. 61 of 75 contained traces of glucan.

RA.712 Analog Soul // You two have been DJing in New York long enough to see a few different eras come and go. How do you feel about the scene right now?

JS: In spite of the past or politics, currently NY is on fire!!! The talent, promoters, venues, record stores (back again now), what's been bubbling in NY the past seven years to me is epic! Right now NY is massive! It deserves the acclaim it had in the '90s, and now, def the acclaim of Berlin as an epicenter of dance music, in my opinion! There's an inclusiveness in sound that I didn't experience at my start in NY in 2001. So to be a part of what's happening now means a lot to me!
