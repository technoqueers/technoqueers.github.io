Friday
Abstract Conditions 1 Year + Dasychira EP Release Party, FaltyDL @ Trans-Pecos // 10 PM - 4 AM // Join an intimate dancefloor moving in new ways to live experimental electronic music. Genre-defying, unknown sounds to be conjured by Dasychira (live), FaltyDL, XGLARE (live), ARIADNE (live), Speak Onion (live), and M1N0M0X. $10
GROOVY GROOVY — STENNY, ANDREA, ASUSU @ RSVP for location // 11 PM - 8 AM // Jump into what feels like a mosh pit without the masculinity, into a sea of bodies vibing so intensely that you'll wonder how nobody ends up getting accidentally punched in the head. StennY, ANDREA, and Asusu will be making their US debuts, joined by Teleself, Akanbi, and Yogic. In the words of GROOVY GROOVY: "IF YOU'RE NOT SWEATING YOU'RE DOING IT WRONG." Adv tix
UNTER with Yan Cook, Stranger, Jaqi Sparro, Volvox @ Secret Location // midnight - 9 AM // A rare poppertunity to catch Yan Cook and Stranger making their North American debuts, joined by Jaqi Sparro and Volvox in our favorite sinful dreamworld. Cool off in the ambient room, with soundscapes by The Long Count Cycle, Adrianna, and Kiddo Sincere b2b Nick Bazzano. Adv tix sold out / limited door tix
Saturday
Deniro, Physical Therapy, Volvox @ Trans-Pecos // midnight - 4 AM // Your first stop is the US debut of Deniro. If you're unfamiliar, indulge in his recent Kairuku EP and his set from Dekmantel 2016. With Physical Therapy and Volvox sharing the bill, this will be four hours of pure fire! Adv tix / $15 door
T E Ch N O Fe M I N is M @ Bossa // 10 PM - 4 AM // The Home Depot is listed as the location but this is happening at Bossa. But could the dancefloor be situated in the tropical plants section, or maybe among the chandeliers? Or in the plumbing aisle, a new take on Club Toilet? For anyone who was missing Bossa, this will be the time to celebrate its reawakening. Sets from Juana, Johann Moon (live), Beta Librae, and Umfang. Free before midnight
The Closet: 2 @ Schimanski // 11 PM - 5 AM // This time, Ladyfag treats us to even more techno, with Carlos Souffront, Vin Sol, Butched, Justin Cudmore, and Techno Queers DJ Ne/Re/A taking the decks. Beware: drinks sold at Manhattan prices bc it's Schimanski. $10 before 11:30 PM / $20 after // For the first 30 people who say "Techno Queers" at the door after 11:30 PM, it'll be only $15. // NY Times: Ladyfag Hosts a New Party in Brooklyn
Twice As Proper @ RSVP for location // 10 PM - 9 AM // Feeling deja vu? That might be because we accidentally listed this event for last Saturday. // No matter where you go for your first stop, we shall converge here for afters. Deep techno delivered by Reggy Van Oers (US debut) and Deepbass, joined by locals Craft, Headliner, Shadi, and Dees. Adv tix
