Two Shell, Shitposting, and One Direction w/ @meetmeintranspecos

By Lucas Gelfond, ZINE MUNCH

A few years ago there was a huge wave of DIY spaces shutting down in every city. There's too many to list off the top of my head, like Silent Barn, Shea Stadium, 285 Kent, and all of those spaces are gone, and not having those places to gather really put the damper on smaller bands. Miraculously Trans-Pecos has survived this whole arc, and I think it's genuinely one of the most important places for building a DIY-oriented community, even though it's a legit venue, like not technically someone's apartment.
