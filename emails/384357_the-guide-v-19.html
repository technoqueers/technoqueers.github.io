(( Mix Of The Week & Artist Profile ))

(( Justin Cudmore ))

With the release of “Crystal” on Honey Soundsystem’s HNYTRX label last May, Justin Cudmore has quickly made a name for himself as a rising star of the Brooklyn techno world. You may not know it, but chances are you’ve heard the slinky retro-tinged banger or one of its remixes work a dancefloor this summer. Its playful acid line, weightless melody, and irresistible thump make it a supremely versatile track, and one that can shift a room’s energy into high gear during house and techno-leaning sets alike. Riding Crystal’s wave of momentum, Cudmore plans to release several new tracks in the coming months, one of which, dubbed “Forget It”, already has Mike Servito calling it his “favorite track of the year” after he weaved it seamlessly into his recent Beats in Space mix and Dekmantel set.

 

Despite the recent buzz, Justin is no newcomer to the scene. As a native of Illinois, Cudmore came of age DJing underground parties in Chicago and writing for the now-defunct blog Little White Earbuds, before relocating to New York several years ago to pursue his DJ career. This mix, which he recorded in 2014 for the LWE podcast series, expertly blends propulsive, wonderfully textured techno together with hypnotic house tracks to create a remarkably cohesive whole that lingers in the mind long after the mix ends. What begins with shimmering ambient melodies gradually gives way to an interplay of dusty grooves, snaking acid lines, air-tight hi-hats, and warm, soulful vocal samples. The transition from the celestial DJ Koze “La Duquesa” (Efdemin’s Cose Cosi Mix) to the burnt-orange groove of Round Two’s “New Day” is particularly smooth, and softens the mood before a series of jackin’ house and darker techno sounds slap the mix with kinetic energy. As the mix rollicks onward, the blissfully deep synth loops of Soul Capsule’s iconic “Lady Science” eclipse the shadowy drum pattern underneath it before the post-rave euphoria of Closer Musik’s “One Two Three” carries the mix to its gentle conclusion.

 

While nearly all DJs can mix two tracks together, Justin’s strength lies in his ability to layer contrasting sonic elements in genuinely exciting ways that never seem forced or predictable. Justin’s style belies a deep musicality, and together with his impressive record collection means you can always expect well mixed, perfectly balanced DJ sets that nail the vibe every time.

You can catch Justin this Saturday at the Remedy party at Aviv, where he plays alongside Berlin-based Annanan. Grab a $10 ticket while it lasts and come out to support the newest addition to Brooklyn’s all-star lineup!
Thursday
Birds of Prey, Borne & Matthew Patterson Curry at The Mission // This event is part of the fundraising concert series organized by The Lot Radio, to help the neighboring church renovate their pipe organ. Birds of Prey, a live electronic collaboration between Grant Aaron, Eric Holmes, Camille Atlay, and Clay Wilson, will thrust you deep into unknown, sublime, gradually-evolving soundscapes. They're joined by Safety Scissors, bringing experimental, eccentric dance and pop music, and Borne, who will immerse us in ambient experimental works infused with house, techno, and dub. Doors & bar at 8 PM, show starts at 8:30 PM.
P.L. at Bossa : Bergsonist (live) , Debbie Djs Dallas, Djs Mig and Nick // Industrial noise techno taking you on a deep inner journey: Bergsonist (live) is joined by Debbie Djs Dallas, Djs Mig and Nick, and a probable special guest appearance from Cienfuegos (of Brooklyn's own BANK Records).
Secrets - Sweat Equity Friends B2b2b2b JX Cannon Akanbi Teleself Stress Special Guests // Make Thursday your sweatiest! Thrown in conjunction with B2b2b2b2b2b insane party by Abby, this roster of local talent will ensure insane levels of energy on both floors of Trans-Pecos. Expect a variety of genres plus a special hardstyle set from Abby b2b Juliana Huxtable.
Friday
DJ Richard * Via App * DJ Dog Dick * Bully Fae * Negashi Armada * Pinky Rose // After this final weekend of shows, the Melrose St. location of Secret Project Robot will close down, so this is your chance to say goodbye. Going from 8 PM - 2 AM, this show is your first stop of the evening. Prominent local DJ and producer DJ Richard is known for his diverse sets infused with a murky mixture of techno, drone, and noise music. He is joined by another local star, Via App, who delivers experimental and outsider techno-centered sets with industrial drums, synths, and heavily abstracted vocals.
Ambient Church // Behold the first gathering of Ambient Church, at the lovely Park Church Co-Op across from Greenpoint's eerie McGorlick Park. Expect an inundation of live sound/film collaborations, crystal singing bowls, incense, candles, and libations. Produced and curated by Brian Sweeny of Body Actualized, who will be adorning us with an ambient set. Doors at 8 PM, show starts at 8:30 PM. Reception with DJs to follow in church basement.
Juan Ramos & Ron Like Hell : Dance To The Beat // Back for another night of delicious acid house beats with a psychedelic disco twist yet again at Bushwick's happiest friendly gay bar. Join Ron Like Hell and his friend, Juan Ramos of CockTail d'Amore-Berlin (a monthy queer party and record label), as they celebrate his latest release on ESP Institute.
Basement Floor Festivus: Stefan Ringer + Miguel Senquiz // Stefan Ringer is here from Atlanta to bring you his blend of house music sourcing from Chicago and Detroit house, Miami bass, and Baltimore club. Joined by Soul 2 Seoul (Turtle Bugg & Phillip Chung), and Miguel Senquiz of Spectral Sound, the dancefloor home of Ghostly International.
WT Records Presents: Bitchin' Locals with Entro Senestre (live), Willie Burns and Scallywag plus Dat Kat in the Bad Room // WT Records is taking over Good Room! Entro Senestre will be giving us his take on atmospheric, lo-fi techno with a live set. Plus DJ sets from label head Willie Burns and Scallywag. Dat Kat of Analog Soul holds down the Bad Room with house vibes all night. This party is FREE with RSVP.
Jurassic Bottom : TryShareATop (R Gamble, Claudia De Châlon, Dream, Sprklbb) // Venture to Gateway for house and techno with a queer crowd and cheap drinks, sets from R Gamble, Claudia De Châlon, Dream, and Sprklbb. Expect dinosaur eggs filled with condoms and candy, and a densely-foggy not-yet-coneptualized-but-certain-to-be-spectacular poppers forest.
Insect Presents: Olin + Ital // Olin is a resident DJ at Smartbar in Chicago whose sets masterfully traverse through genres to create breathtaking narratives. He's joined by local DJ and producer Ital, known for releasing deep, lush, and hypnotic tracks. The INSECT crew will be opening with sets from Sedan Sedan and Kneena.
Saturday
Remedy with Annanan Live and Justin Cudmore // Strap yourselves in for a techno-infused evening at DIY venue Aviv, now featuring AC! Here from Berlin, Annanan (live) will immerse you in futuristic, robotic sounds stitched together with melancholy but passionate melodies. Justin Cudmore will be bringing his blend of techno laced with snaking acid lines and bouncy grooves. Rounding out the night will be Remedy's Maroje, who will be slapping you in the face with thick bass lines and weirdo vocals, and Amourette, who will serve up fresh, hot and spicy electro.
99 Sense - Warehouse Pool Party #2 // A Bushwick warehouse pool party from 3 PM til 6 AM (or later), featuring a stacked lineup of local artists. Expect a mix of techno, disco, house, afrobeat, footwork, and dancehall to keep you grooving (and swimming) til the early morning. Don't do too many downers while you're in the pool -- there are probably no life guards on duty.
Synaptic 04: Claude Young, Jake Reif, Heather Heart, Scott Murakami at Secret Brooklyn Location TBA // Detroit techno legend Claude Young, known for fearlessly manipulating tracks and transitions during his sets, is joined by local underground DJs Jake Reif, Heather Heart, and Scott Murakami. 
KUNST // Susanne Bartsch and Dreamhouse saunter over to Output to transform it into decadent club kid utopia with probably zero bros. Sets by Lauren Flax, Amber Valentine, Bridget Marie ZaZaZaDisco, and Colby B plus a slew of hosts and live performances.
EYE x EAR: Afterparty // Ellie Herring / Dot / Baltra / Truant / Davis Galvin B2B Will Martin / TJ Pulliam // EYE x EAR is a free, all-ages exhibit of synesthetic work between vision and audition exploring sensual experience (happening at Powrplnt from 7-11 PM). The afterparty will be similarly mind-bending, featuring sets from Ellie Herring, Dot, Baltra, Truant, and Davis Galvin b2b Will Martin.
Sunday
Dream 2 Science w/ Turtle Bugg, Shawn Dub, The Duchess, & Unscented DJ // Spend your Sunday swaying to smooth, deep, and sexy house music with Dream 2 Science. Helping warm up this garden affair will be Turtle Bugg, The Duchess, Shawn Dub, and Unscented DJ.
Soul Summit Music Festival // Fort Greene's long-running free outdoor festival is back this Sunday, this time featuring Melba Moore and a dress-in-all-white-clothing theme. This is a community-focused, family-friendly house music festival overflowing with positive vibes; it is the opposite of you standing in the corner riding out your k hole in a Sith robe.
Nights Over BK - W. Jeremy/ Michael Magnan/ Christy Love/ Butched at Output // Between this event and KUNST on Saturday, this might be Output's gayest weekend ever! Ass-shaking house music for the queers brought to you by W. Jeremy, Michael Magnan, Christy Love, and Butched.
Cloud City EP Release Party // Stop by Bossa to celebrate the release of Will Martin's first solo EP, out on Discovery Recordings! He's joined by Free Magic, Keith Edward, and Beto Cravioto.
