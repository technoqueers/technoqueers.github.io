Techno Queers NY
(( MIX OF THE WEEK // LENA WILLIKENS ))

Being a longtime resident DJ teaches you how to stand out from the pack. Cologne’s Lena Willikens is one such special selector who has very much made Salon Des Amateurs in Dusseldorf her own. She is also part of the Comeme Records crew with the likes of Barnt. For them she hosts a regular radio show, and for them she has released her brooding, industrially bleak techno sounds.

Lena’s influences come from well outside the normal canon: proto-techno, industrial boogie, synthesized disco, outlier house and robot jack all characterize her work, including this set she has served up for Dekmantel. Expect one hour of unusual grooves, unsettling atmospheres and metallic textures that are abstract and dehumanised, taking you to a raw and visceral place that is hugely intoxicating. This mix has received the highest praises from the folks at Dekmantel -- it has also been melting brains over on our side of the pond.

Thursday
SISTER NYC // After their launch in Berlin, SISTER is now gracing our shores for their second installment! Prepare yourself for this roster of insanely talented female/non-binary-identifying artists. Like Techno Queers NY, SISTER exists at the intersection of social justice and electronic music. Come for the music, stay for the solidarity.
Friday
Aphotic x Lost Soul Enterprises present: Cassegrain & Dungeon Acid // Plug in for a wild ride of feverish, tunneling techno and sharp acid -- somewhere, of course, in the depths of Bushwick. This is going to be the quintessential warehouse vibe of the weekend, so wear your nicest gown.
Omar S/ John Tejada/ O B Ignitt at Output and Discwoman: Kim Anh/ Analog Soul/ Katie Rex in The Panther Room // Of course we know Output can be... offputting. But with Discwoman bringing their crew to the smaller, more intimate Panther Room, we might just have ourselves a night! If you feel like journeying into the main room to see Omar S and John Tejada, go for it, but don't say we didn't warn you.
Saturday
GROOVY GROOVY pres. WORLDWIDE GUESTS / AKANBI / JX CANNON // Get ready for some sweat-inducing, head-banging, left-of-center techno served up by Akanbi, JX CANNON, and a secret special guest! Hosted by Whatever 21, with installations by Nitemind Creative, you can expect equally immersive sonic and visual atmospheres.
2MR Presents Sage Caswell x Earth Boys x Mike Simonetti at Black Flamingo // Apparently this is the weekend of sequels!  After the success of the first 2MR party with Stefan Ringer, they’re doing another showcase at Black Flamingo. Expect intimate basement vibes and some really tasty tacos being consumed above you while you dance.
Still thirsty?
FRIDAY // Industry of Machines presents: Nicuri, Person of Interest, Gaul Plus, JM de Frias // Though typically techno, Industry of Machines is ringing in the spring with a lineup edging more towards the groove-inducing, deep house end of the spectrum. There will still be some techno in the mix, and it's at Bossa, so just grab a vodka mate and enjoy yourself!
SATURDAY // The Spectrum Presents: Nights of the Templar w/ Dreamhouse // The colorful, ridiculous, and legendary queer party has finally found itself a brand new venue. Not techno, but that’s only half of our name! This is the place to let your sexuality fly, crawl, and stumble around. Expect a performance, on stage and off.
