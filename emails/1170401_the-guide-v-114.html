Gay Black Men Helped Create EDM. Why Do Straight White Men Dominate It? // Longtime music journalist Zel McCarthy, who is gay, notes that the business model hasn't changed “because the people running the business are straight men.”

One in five UK festivalgoers sexually assaulted or harassed, says YouGov survey // Of the 1,188 people who took part in the survey, 22 percent said they had been harassed or assaulted, with the percentage rising to 30 percent for female festivalgoers. Forty-three percent of people under 40 said they'd faced some kind of unwanted sexual behaviour. The most common type of incident was "unwelcome and forceful dancing." The survey also found that only two percent of cases had been reported to the police.

Club Chai's DJs on the power of DIY nightlife // Club Chai happens every two or three months in Oakland; finding the right venue is a constant challenge. Artists’ warehouses have long provided vital infrastructure for the city’s disjointed nightlife scene, but in the wake of a deadly fire at the Ghost Ship warehouse in December 2016, a crackdown on venues without permits has limited their options. “Honestly, right now we’re not sure where the next one is going to be,” says Sarkissian. “But it’s exciting,” adds Canoğullari, “because it seems like our crowd is willing to follow us around.”
