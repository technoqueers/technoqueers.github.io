Techno Queers NY
(( MIX OF THE WEEK ))
(( Matrixxman ))
Augmented - Homesick LP, Ghostly International (2015)
Venetian Mask - Amulet EP, Spectral Sound (2014)

He refers to himself as a resurgent techno atavist, and his sounds fall somewhere between Chicago acid house and classic Detroit techno. He has unleashed a storm of EPs in recent years, finding himself a home in tastemaking imprint Dekmantel. You can catch him this Saturday at Wrecked x The Bunker PRIDE!
 
Thursday
Good Room presents Delta Funktionen // R Gamble // Justin Cudmore // Having released on Delsin with Chicago, Detroit, electro and Italo influences, Delta Funktionen is known for using analogue and digital tools in exciting new ways that bare resemblance to the past whilst always looking forwards. He'll be joined by R Gamble of Lost Soul Enterprises, Der Panther (live), and Justin Cudmore (who has just released his Crystal 12" via Honey Soundsystem).
FIST ft Robert Armani / Dischetto / Matthew Arkell (Gatekeeper) / Chicken / Word of Command // Check out this new night at Mercury Lounge, which strives to harness the power of techno to celebrate otherness and fight the status quo (yes, events hosted at venues in Manhattan can still fight the status quo). FIST brings you the banging sounds of Robert Armani, Dischetto, Matthew Arkell, Chicken, and Word of Command.
Friday
Ostbahnhof Pride: Collapse Factory w/ Population 1/ Mystic Bill/ Lauren Flax/ Kimyon/ Victor Rodriguez/ Mark Louque/ Sprklbb // The legendary Detroit native Terrence Dixon (Population 1) brings his deep atmospheres and minimal techno sounds to The Gateway, a new 3-floor venue named for its proximity to the Gates Ave. J Train (the oldest continuously-operating subway station in the MTA's history, since you needed to know that). He'll be joined by Mystic Bill, Lauren Flax, Kimyon, Victor Rodriguez, Mark Louque, and Sprklbb.
Basement Floor Festivus: Patricia \\ The Duchess // Patricia will use his hiss loaded, dazzlingly dissonant, plodding yet pulsating rhythms to plunge Bossa deep into an oceanic abyss. He's joined by The Duchess and Soul 2 Seoul for an intimate evening fueled by local talent.
Submission: L. Sangre, Worshippers, Ciarra Black at Bad Room // Strap on your leather gear for this industrial-leaning evening of sin, featuring L. Sangre, Worshippers (live), and Ciarra Black. And then step out of the void (into the event running simultaneously at Good Room) to catch the prolific Intergalactic Gary, joined by DJ Overdose, Cosmic Force (live), and Willie Burns.
Deep Thoughts [Dakini9 / Galcher Lustwerk / Andrew Devlon] // If you are thirsty for house music, bathe in the dreamy soundscapes and deep grooves of Galcher Lustwerk, Dakini9, and Andrew Devlon.
Saturday
Art in the Air // Music Underground IV // Who ever said Aphotic hated the sunshine? Start your Saturday off with an immersive daytime festival celebrating local street artists and underground dance music! Artists: Painting Live, Meres One, Kwue Molly, Zeh Palito, Gumshoe, Aom, Uta Brauser, Fumero, and Lunar New Year. Music: Sam Jaspersohn, Chris Luzz, Cry Baby, and Dao.
Art in the Air Afterparty Ft. Stephanie Sykes Craft Clay Wilson // If you arrive early for the daytime portion of the festival, it may be possible to spend 24 consecutive hours dancing your face off at the same venue. Late night techno ceremonies brought to you by Stephanie Sykes, Craft, Clay Wilson, Oliver Caine, and Dani Lehman.
Wrecked x The Bunker PRIDE | Matrixxman/ Jeffrey Sfire/ WRECKEDnyc/ DJ Minx/ Carlos Souffront/ Octo Octa/ Bryan Kasenic // This is probably the only time of the year in which we will tell you to go to Output. Get ready for an unforgettable night of dance floor bliss, brought to you by Matrixxman, Jeffrey Sfire, WRECKEDnyc (Ryan Smith & Ron Like Hell), DJ Minx, Carlos Souffront, Octo Octa, and Bryan Kasenic. A portion of the proceeds will be donated to Equality Florida's fund on behalf of the victims of the recent tragedy.
Fhloston Paradise // This Fifth Element-themed party needs to be on your radar! Expect a variety of local talent spanning across genres, taking over both floors of Trans-Pecos: Santa Muerte, DJ Kala, Night Doll, Quest?onmarc, DV-i, Gooddroid b2b Gossip Gurl, Zehno, Katie Rex, Mary S., Nick Bazzano, Images, Peal, and Bouffant. Proceeds from this event will also be donated to the Equality Florida Pulse Victims Fund.
Time Corp with Max McFerren x John Barera x Will Martin at Black Flamingo // If you'd prefer a more intimate, local, and taco-focused evening of techno, head to Black Flamingo for Max McFerren, John Barera, and Will Martin.
