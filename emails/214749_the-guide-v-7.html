((MIX OF THE WEEK))
(( Frankie Bones ))
Credited with bringing rave culture to New York and creating the template for early NY techno, Frankie Bones is not to be missed this Friday.
Friday
JACK DEPT. NYC / AARON CLARK / HARUKA / VOLVOX / JOHN BARERA // Aaron Clark and Volvox will be transmitting vibes straight from Club Toilet.
Cultivated Sound & Purple Trax present: Frankie Bones // Demanding freaks, couture, and dark figures in motion.
The Bunker with Moritz Von Oswald and Hugo Ball // Joining our beloved Bunker crew will be Dub Techno pioneer Moritz Von Oswald and Hugo Ball's Nathan Drew Larsen and Justin Long.
Sublimate & Ruse present: Jayda G, Madteo & Entro Senestre // Want house music? Catch guests Jayda G and Madteo. Then immerse yourself in live techno from Entro Senestre, head of newly formed BANK Records.
Saturday
CHOCK A BLOCK - Day Party // How could we not include a free chocolate-fueled daytime warehouse party celebrating seven DJs from local labels and crews? Expect sun-soaked house vibes from William Roy (99¢ Dreams), Jen Orlando (REMEDY), MPL (Conditioner), MDG (Division Point Industries), Nikki (The Lot Radio), Jordan James (Supply NY), and Moe.Bpm (Good-Natured Sound).
Luke Hess & How To Kill Records: What's In The Box? // Expect amazing selections from this lineup representing Detroit's new but deeply-rooted techno label How To Kill Records.
OP 001: Nonotak (US Debut) / Lee Bannon b2b Dedekind Cut / Volvox / Sporting Life / Aquarian / Cry Baby // For OP 001, we're transforming a furniture warehouse into a popup audio-visual playground, bringing together a slew of international artists & performers for a onetime experiential event.
Tunnel Vision w/ Antenes / Ital / DJ Wey // A fogged-out journey from the lighter to the darker side of techno, closed out by a 2-hr set from Antenes.
Sunday
Sundays on The Roof | Function/ Daniel Bell/ Bryan Kasenic // Grab a summer cocktail at this afternoon rooftop affair, featuring Function (Sandwell District), Daniel Bell (minimal techno pioneer), and Bryan Kasenic (founder of The Bunker).
Perc/ Donor/ Mary Yuzovskaya at Output // Splitting her time between New York, Berlin, and Moscow, Mary Yuzovskaya (Halcyon the Shop) somehow manages to be a local underground favorite. Dance away Sunday evening after soaking up the afternoon sunshine.
