+ Bathhouses +

+ Man’s memories of AIDS hitting the NYC bathhouse scene goes viral [Queerty] "​When I arrived in 1976, sex was as easy as eating a ham sandwich. There was no shame in it — no reason for any. We were living inside the golden age of homosexuality and free love in the greatest city in all the world without knowing that’s what it was. You could have sex four times before noon and still make your afternoon audition."

"The Club Baths attracted a younger clientele, while The Mount Morris Baths in Harlem had a greater percentage of Black patrons. There was also the Wall Street Sauna, the East Side Sauna, and the famed Continental Baths, which later became Plato’s Retreat."

"​It’s worth noting that most of Europe allowed bathhouses to remain open, where they flourished. The establishments typically stocked free condoms and lube and offered safer-sex information. Today, sexual health clinics often do outreach work at the bigger bathhouses, including HIV testing. Health workers believe they reach people in such venues who rarely or never visit a clinic."
