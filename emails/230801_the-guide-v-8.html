(( Mix of the Week ))
(( Abe Duque ))
Abe Duque ft. Blake Baxter - What Happened? (Abe's Original Mix)
Abe Duque & Blake Baxter - Acid (Old School mix)

His surname is pronounced “Du-kay”. He jokingly describes himself as one of the "few producers still licensed to use the 303". But with a career that started in the early days of house at New York's notorious Limelight, Abe Duque has been at the head of the acid house and underground techno table since his earliest releases way back in 1993. His first tracks were influenced by the madness of the Limelight's club-kid audience, later publicised by films and books like Party Monster and Clubland Confidential. Catch him this Friday at Spectrum!
Thursday
Cosmin TRG + John Barera and Will Martin (live) with Scallywag // Taking cues from 90s era leftfield electronics, warehouse techno, the pioneers of early lab recordings and minimal film scores, Cosmin TRG has created his own brand of modern, textural sonics in a contemporary context. Free before midnight with RSVP.
Friday
 
The Spectrum Presents: Nights of the Templar w/ Dreamhouse // This is your first stop on Friday night: bring a lqqk (bc it's Spectrum), dance your face off to the legendary Abe Duque, and chain smoke indoors in this classy as fuck underground venue, named after our favorite vasodilator.
Unter x Discwoman // This is your second stop, or maybe your first stop if you manage to not leave your apartment until after 2 AM. Unter's 1-year bday celebration, held in collaboration with Discwoman, features a lineup packed to the brim with local underground stars. Get ready for the return of XxX Unter punch, and for a queer techno dancefloor unlike any other.
Saturday
Industry of Machines presents: Oliver Ho, White Visitation, Witness, AxFx // If you want a musically extravagant evening in an intimate setting, head to Bossa for Oliver Ho, the UK techno legend making his long awaited return to NY. He'll be joined by White Visitation, the breakthrough Mexican producer making his U.S. live debut.
Alone Together Vol. 9 w/ Jus-Ed, Mary Yuzovskaya, Beta Librae // If you're in the mood for a house-leaning evening and just want to sweat it out, strap yourselves in for the fabulously selected sounds of the Jus-Ed, yet another legendary DJ gracing our dancefloors this weekend. He'll be joined by our underground favorites Mary Yuzovskaya and Beta Librae.
