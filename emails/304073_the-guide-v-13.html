(( MIX OF THE WEEK ))

(( Ancient Methods ))

 

In this Boiler Room mix, Michael Wollenhaupt a.k.a. Ancient Methods, delivers a dark, churning progression of machine-driven techno, replete with whirring percussion, grinding low-end distortion, and bleak melodies peppered over chopped synth lines. Its sheer rawness plunges the listener into a hyper-industrial landscape, and dancefloor functionality is an evident driving force in the way he layers tracks and builds momentum. Yet, as hushed vocal samples, organic instrumentation, and warped New Wave remixes emerge, it becomes clear that his goal is not so much to obliterate dancefloors as it is to shake their foundations and lock dancers in a hypnotic trance. It is the seamless way in which he weaves these divergent forces together, creating a lush and immersive whole, that renders Ancient Methods unique among the sea of black-clad Berlin DJs and producers.

Last year, he told Boiler Room, “An exciting DJ set, though, is never pleasing the crowd by giving them what they are used to hearing, but rather taking them to something that might be new to them. I see techno as a great tool to build a frame that is not only designed for better accessibility but also for a greater physical, bodily way of experiencing music. The magick happens inside the frame.”

You can expect that Ancient Methods will bring his brand of blackened techno to Bunker x Unter this weekend, from 4-8 AM this Saturday morning. Fasten your seatbelts.
