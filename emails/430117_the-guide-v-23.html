(( artist of the week ))
(( DJ Minx ))

DJ Minx, a.k.a. Jennifer Witcher may keep a low profile, but you better believe she is no amateur. The Detroit native and Women on Wax (W.O.W) label head began her DJ career in the mid-90s after “listening to Derrick May beat folks into submission” at the famed Music Institute of Detroit. From 1996-1998, she ran Deep Space Radio on local Detroit station WGPR, which she co-hosted with techno godfathers Juan Atkins, Kevin Saunderson, and Derrick May. In 2000, she was featured in Movement precursor DEMF’s first ever lineup, and the following year launched Women on Wax Recordings -- an all-female DJ collective and label that she continues to operate out of Detroit. Over the past decade, Minx has played consistently at respected parties across the country, and most recently launched W.O.W. sub-imprint W.O.W. B.A.M. (Women On Wax Bangin’ Ass Music).

Despite her well-established reputation, Minx has developed something of a queer underground cult following in the past few years due to several key performances at Honey Soundsystem’s SF pride party, Honcho, and Detroit’s infamous Club Toilet.

This mix, recorded live at Hot Mass in Pittsburgh, highlights just why Minx’s popularity has skyrocketed recently among techno-leaning homos. Punchy kicks and hypnotic, looping bass lines drive her sets forward like a freight train, while deep, soulful melodies and the occasional vocal sample supply a jackin’ party vibe throughout. Her style is sub bass-heavy and unrelenting, and she isn’t afraid to jolt the dance floor with fresh doses of energy via obscure, ravey house tracks. For instance, Minx drops Aril Brikha’s jungle-flavored “Groove La Chord” in the latter third of this set, a track whose warped snares and frenetic claps crescendo irresistibly towards Chuck Robert’s iconic “My House” refrain of “House is an uncontrollable desire to jack your body!”  During such moments, one can imagine the sweaty bodies writhing in ecstasy, visible only through slices of strobe lights, while Minx grins slyly in satisfaction.

This Saturday, DJ Minx will be serving piping hot tracks on the patio from 5-8pm at the 3rd edition of Bunker X Unter. Don’t miss out!
