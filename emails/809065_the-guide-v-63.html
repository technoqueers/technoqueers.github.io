Thursday
Dissolution 005 @ The Gateway // 10 PM - 4 AM // Local favorites vveiss, John Fay, Wawa, & Xiorro. Free
Friday... 9 parties on our radar
Banquet: part 4 @ The Dreamhouse aka The Spectrum // 10 PM - 6 AM // A sex positive space featuring a clothes check and sets from Michael Magnan & A Village Raid. Respect boundaries, no phones... enjoy the feast :) Door: $20 b4 midnight / $25 after / $ goes toward sustaining this queer space.
The Party by Ostbahnhof: New New Wave @ Lot 45 // 10 PM - 4 AM // If you're looking for queer vibes but aren't feeling Banquet, Ostbahnhof is serving vibrant house music from Tahl K, Char, & DJ Help, as well as live performances. $ goes toward local LGBTQI orgs.
The Gateway Presents // 11PM - 5 AM // Probably the hardest techno among the local lineups taking the decks tonight. FBI Warning (DJ), Auspex, Ne/Re/A $10
Plastic Dreams @ Tilt BK // 10 PM - 4 AM // Lighter on the brain pummeling, a bit more variety: Katie Rex, NK Badtz Maru, Wawa, J Sparro, & Logan G. Free
Teething @ Baby's All Right // 11 PM - 4 AM // Want to check out a new party? Teething v1 presents DJ Spider, Young Male, & DJ Wey at Baby's All Right. Tix
Metamorph @ Jupiter Disco // Lo-fi & atmospheric selections from White Material's Galcher Lustwerk, for 4 hours! Don't spill your fancy cocktail. Free w/ RSVP
Anthony Naples and Analog Soul @ BNCC // 10 PM - 4 AM // Left-field house & techno from Analog Soul & Anthony Naples, as if we didn't already have enough jaw-dropping local talent on the decks this evening.
John Tejada, Akanbi, Olga, Vesna @ The Paper Box // 11 PM - 5 AM // John Tejada graces us with subtler, more melodic techno traversing genre boundaries. He's joined by Akanbi, Olga, & Vesna. Tix
Adult Night Care @ Schimanski // 10 PM - 6 AM // A relatively rare chance to catch Northern Electronics' Abdulla Rashim, joined by Arthur Kimskii, Modest Glesman, & Maŕa. Drinks are overpriced & this venue's Friday night crowd is usually terrible, so bring friends & come here for the music. Tix
Saturday
★ ★ ★ Fourth World 2K17 ★ ★ ★ @ North Brooklyn Location Revealed to Ticket Holders Only // 2 PM - 8 AM // An 18-hour bender with free hot dogs, live performance & body art surprises, lighting by Kip Davis & Nitemind, vegan options, plenty of space, & literally the best fb event description ever written. This is a dazzling showcase of local techno titans & probably the only decent way of celebrating July 4th.
