(( mix of the week ))
(( Developer ))

Adrian Sandoval, aka Developer, prefers to keep a low profile in spite of being a prolific stateside techno producer, clocking in at over 40 releases. He co-founded and managed the first dedicated techno distribution company on the west coast (Entity Music Distribution), and now runs his own label Modularz as well as Developer Archive for his solo work.

Originally from LA, he was first exposed to techno at backyard parties in the 90's, prior to the advent of CDJs. He comes from the same scene as Silent Servant and Santiago Salazar, and he now splits his time between LA and Berlin. He often plays over 40 tracks per hour across three turntables, aided by computers and controllers, to deliver unique and exhilarating sets.

This Saturday he plays alongside one of our favorites from the local underground, Mary Yuzovskaya.

Say "techno queers" at the door for $10 off cover.
