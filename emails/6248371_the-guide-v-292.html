Nick Cave: Forothermore

Exhibiition at the Guggenheim Museum

The exhibition covers the entire breadth of the artist’s career, and features sculpture, installation, video, and rarely seen early works. The exhibition title is a neologism, a new word that reflects the artist’s lifelong commitment to creating space for those who feel marginalized by dominant society and culture—especially working class communities and queer people of color.

Gay Bathhouse Midtowne Spa Los Angeles Closes after 50 Years

By Paulo Murillo, WeHo Times

“Whatever people may think about gay bathhouses, this was an amazing, eclectic, erotic, nonstop sex positive and Queer sanctuary that will be missed by many. There are a few other local places, but certainly nothing like it as far as the diversity of customers and sheer size in California. Sad day.”

San Francisco's new record shop in the Tenderloin

By Jordan Michelman, Datebook

Dark Entries Records is a reverent platform for the work of Cowley, who at last is being recognized as one of San Francisco’s great composers. “For a gay man who suffered from AIDS, whose music sat in boxes for decades after his death, the depth and range of his sounds are incredible.”
