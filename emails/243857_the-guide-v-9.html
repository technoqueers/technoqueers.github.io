Techno Queers NY
((MIX OF THE WEEK))
(( White Visitation ))
Nicolas Guerrero will bathe you in his hypnotic, subdued blend of techno tonight. He describes DJ Shadow's Endtroducing as his "favorite record in history," a major influence during his formative years, as was The Avalanches's Since I Left You. He also draws from artists such as Tim Sweeney, Rhythm & Sound (Mark Ernestus & Moritz Von Oswald), Theo Parrish, Developer, and Kevin Drumm.
Thursday
Terekke // White Visitation // Beta Librae MOVED to Good Room // Terekke and Beta Librae join White Visitation for an evening of mesmerizing and textured techno at Good Room.
Purple Trax Label Night Vol XI: Will DiMaggio / Ball / See Other / Akanbi b2b Teleself // Will DiMaggio, See Other (live), Ball (live), Akanbi B2B Teleself, Logan Takahashi, and Erez Avissar bring you a house-leaning, sweat-inducing, foggy affair at Bossa.
Friday
Friday Night Live: Tropic Of Cancer/ Broken English Club/ Nikola Baytala // Your Saturday night starts at 5 PM: make sure to catch Tropic of Cancer (live), Broken English Club (live), and Nikola Baytala, who will be bringing mind-bending experimental techno to Output's rooftop.
The Bunker x Unsound Festival NY: Cliff Lothar / Erika / Crimes of the Future / Beau Wanze / Further Reductions // As part of its dislocation series, Unsound festival is visiting NY this weekend, tonight teaming up with The Bunker to bring us this obviously stacked lineup: Cliff Lothar, Erika, Damián Romero, Timothy J. Fairplay, Further Reductions, and Nihal Ramchandani.
Sound Warrior x Discwoman: Jenifa Mayanja, Dakini 9, Demian Licht & Umfang // Sound Warrior and Discwoman are taking over our favorite fogged-out nook, flooding your brain with an infusion of deep house and techno: Jenifa Mayanja, Dakini 9, Demian Licht, and Umfang.
Saturday
Sustain-Release x Unsound Festival NY: Zenker Brothers / Jlin / DJ Richard / Via App / Greg Z & FlucT / Olivia // Sustain-Release and Unsound festival bring you this weekend's third consecutive evening of mind-blowing lineups at Good Room, this time drifting toward dreamier, more experimental soundscapes. Don't miss Zenker Brothers (Dario & Marco Zenker), Jlin, DJ Richard, Via App, FlucT x Greg Zifcak, and Olivia.
Worldwave — Patrick Russell — Anton Maiovvi — Max McFerren // Anton Maiovvi, known for crafting sounds influenced by synth-pop, hi-NRG, and horror soundtracks, is joined by local underground favorites Patrick Russell and Max McFerren for Sisterjam at Bossa.
Abstract Conditions: W00dy / Isabella / Compactor / Auspex / m1n0m0x // This underground lineup of genre misfits will thrust you into unknown and experimental musical territories: W00dy (live), Isabella (live), COMPACTOR (live), Auspex, and m1n0m0x.
Sunday
The Bunker x Unsound Festival New York x Club To Club present The Italian New Wave: Ninos du Brasil / Greg Fox / Vaghe Stelle // Pull yourself out of your typical Sunday solipsistic void: this mysterious, genre-defying lineup is not to be missed. Ninos Du Brasil, Vaghe Stelle, Greg Fox, and Club to Club Soundsystem will close out the Unsound festival in NY this weekend, happening 2-8 PM in the backyard of Trans-Pecos.
Distant Signal Presents: J. Albert // The heady, ethereal vibes of Jeals and J. Albert (live) will make you melt into the dance floor at this cozy Sunday evening showcase. They're joined by Distant Signal residents Euclid, SWIM, and Fast Times.
