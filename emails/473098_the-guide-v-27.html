(( mix of the week ))
(( Octo Octa ))

Brooklyn-based Octo Octa (Maya Bouldry-Morrison) is an unassuming tour-de-force. Over the course of her five-year career, she has carved out a retro-tinged, melodic house sound through roughly ten releases on respected labels such as 100% Silk, Running Back, Argot, and DeepBlak. As a DJ, she regularly delivers innovative, gear-driven live sets and has played some of Europe’s best clubs including Berlin’s Panorama Bar and Frankfurt’s Robert Johnson.

Since coming out as trans a little over a year ago, Bouldry-Morrison has sought to address queerness more directly through her work. This began before her transition was public knowledge with 2013’s full-length album “Between Two Selves”, a record that explores a deep personal struggle with identity. She recently told RA: “there's something more exciting about performing and appearing physically the way you've been wanting to for a long time.”

This mix, entitled “Get in My Room” and released as part of the Ghostly International podcast series, is an infectiously joyous throwback to the sounds of her native Chicago. Warm, soulful house grooves bounce and jack, while ecstatic vocal samples and gospel-inspired piano melodies provide delights throughout.

Octo Octa will be playing this Friday at Sublimate, alongside Oakland-based AYBEE, and local favorites Turtle Bugg and Sagostky. See you there!
