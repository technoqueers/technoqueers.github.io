Harm Reduction for DIY venues

This edition of the guide is dedicated to Chelsea Faith Dolan, aka Cherushii, and Johnny Igaz, aka Nackt, artists from the label 100% Silk who were scheduled to perform at Ghost Ship and lost their lives on the night of the fire. 

Cherushii - Nightsteps (SILK081)

Nackt - Scheherazade (City Mix)
Thursday
Amplify - Discwoman, NYC @ Knockdown Center // A workshop from 6:30-8 PM, lead by a team of fabulous folks who will literally help you scream loud and clear, for many reasons and for no reason. All donations will benefit the Standing Rock Sioux and the Oakland Fire Relief Fund. Open to everyone.
John FM / Huerco S / Lis Dalton / Hank Jackson @ Bossa // Pregayme with John FM's recent RA mix & real af interview re: Detroit. He's joined by Huerco S, Lis Dalton, and Hank Jackson.
Friday
Abstract Conditions: Cc / Deflector / Snaykhunt + more @ Trans-Pecos // Abstract Conditions will be making a donation to the NYC chapter of NOW (National Organization for Women), matching the dollar amount collected at the door. Til 4 AM, lose your mind to the new, unfiltered sounds of CC (live), Deflector (live), Snaykhunt (live), Champagne Sequins (live), Slow Tongued Beauty (live), rtype, and Nick Bazzano, with visuals by M1N0M0X.
Basement Floor Festivus Gets Wrecked @ Bossa // If you're not feeling up for experimental vibes, proper ass shaking at Bossa is your backup, compliments of Wrecked and Soul to Seoul. This edition of Basement Floor Festivus is gonna be a little extra gay since Wrecked is headlining.
N&W Party #1: Volvox / Alex From Queens / Soren Roi @ RSVP for location - TBA Brooklyn // Soren Roi will start the night with a live modular set, followed by extended DJ sets from Alex from Queens and Volvox, going til 7 AM. Either stay all night, or swing by for Volvox's obviously epic closing set. She actually *just* dropped her RA mix, so stop everything you're doing and push play if you have not yet indulged!
Saturday
The Closet @ Schimanski // Thank you Ladyfag & crew for queering the fuck out of Schimanski til 5AM, and for inviting Spencer Parker to headline this madness! He's a regular guest DJ at Berghain boasting a unique blend of house and techno, running Work Them Records, and releasing heavily on REKIDS. He's joined by LA's Kim Anh, as well as our own Fatherhood (Physical Therapy & Michael Magnan) and Butched. Also: one of Spencer Parker's aliases is Brotherhood, and he is DJing this event alongside Fatherhood. Email us your conspiracy theories.

If you're not digging The Closet and also happen to be a gay dude with extra money to blow on a steep cover charge, walk over to Output for trusty, rusty, poppers-drenched #HorseMeatMarketDisco, tonight featuring Tad Haes in Main Room and The Carry Nation in Panther Room (til 6 AM).
CHASM is our approved escape plan if Schimanski still ends up feeling like an oppressive place to dance, in spite of Ladyfag's best efforts. CHASM is a series of events 12/7 to 12/10 bringing together some of our finest performing and visual artists in a new, intimate underground space (RSVP for location), and tonight is the finale, featuring live sets from Malory and Plebeian, and DJ sets from Lauren Flax, The Long Count Cycle, and Maŕa. Visuals by JJ Brine, Metagasm, and Videopunks, plus a performance by The Oracle (til 4 AM).

Honestly, this Saturday is overcrowded with solid techno lineups playing for intimate venues. (Is this actually a problem?) Keep these on your radar in case our selections this evening don't quite do it for you:

Actual Size w/ BMG, Romulo Del Castillo, Nick Klein, Ken Meier @ Trans-Pecos // Til 4 AM, $10 before midnight.

Powell (debut of live A/V show), Silent Servant, Certain Creatures @ TBA Brooklyn - 395 Wythe Ave. // Til 4 AM. Say password "techno queers" at the door for reduced cover.

Confused House - Via App (live / album release party), Stallone the reducer (live), Wetware (live), Bookworms (live) @ Bossa // Til 4 AM, free before midnight.

Synaptic: Morphology, Patrick Russell, Alex from Queens, Jake Reif, L Genec @ The Gateway // Til 5 AM. Half of proceeds from ticket sales will be donated to the Oakland Fire Relief Fund.
Wednesday, 12/14
BROMO @ Bossa // Techno Queers resident DJ Ne/Re/A plays this edition of BROMO alongside Butched! This is fogged-out faggotry at its finest, your midweek fantasy shared with your chosen family.
