The 11th Annual New York Dance Parade & Festival @ 21st & Broadway // Parade 1-3 PM, DanceFest in Tompkins Sq. Park 3-7 PM // Parade & protest featuring over 150 dance groups. It's the world's largest & most diverse parade of its kind, originating in 2006 in support of a lawsuit seeking to overturn NYC's Cabaret (No Dancing) Law. Among the Grand Marshals this year is Frankie Bones, pioneer of techno widely credited with bringing rave culture to the U.S. (he'll also be DJing an afterparty at Nublu).
