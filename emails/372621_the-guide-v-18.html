Techno Queers NY
Thursday
Pinch, Graphs, Aquarian, True Nature // Pinch brings an otherworldly mix of deep, dark broken-beat techno rhythms, seismic UK-dubstep, grime, dancehall, and future-dub. He brings along Graphs, Aquarian, and True Nature for a dubby night of exquisite musical selections.
Synthicide // Synth-driven sets from Jeffo!, DJ Omac, Fade Kainer, and Andi. Event thrown in conjunction with the Nowhere to Run Festival, celebrating the post-punk community through the years and around the world in NYC.
Femmequerade Ball // This party is taking over Good Room and turning it into a specifically queer/femme-positive space! It’s co-hosted by Brujas, the Bronx-based all-woman skater crew (recently profiled in the NY Times), as well as an inclusive electronic music collective known as Sister, and a satirical “art-bro” duo called Hot Schmucks. Door tickets are $18 for cis straight white men and $15 for everyone else.
Friday
Confused House // A pioneer of the NYC techno scene known for crossing between techno and industrial, Adam X promises to deliver a hard-hitting set punctuated by ambient and minimal sounds that somehow fall perfectly into place. He's joined by Antenes, one of our local favorites known for delivering eclectic techno sets that drift into experimental and psychedelic realms.
Firehouse presents Heat It Up! Kim Ann Foxman with special guest Greg Wilson, Shaun J Wright plus Love Tempo in the Bad Room // Kim Ann Foxman, formerly of Hercules and Love Affair and currently running Firehouse Recordings, kicks off her residency at Good Room alongside UK dance music legend Greg Wilson, who will be treating us to a reel to reel set. Expect a relentless barrage of danceable tunes, including disco, unapologetic tech-house, and more.
Occupy The Disco at Black Flamingo // Occupy the Disco, a collective comprised of Tad Haes, DJ RuBot, and Josh Appelbaum, delivers sets incorporating the sounds of dance music's gay past, as well as sounds of the future, so expect a mix of disco and techno! They are taking over The Black Flamingo with guest DJ Eric Bloom. Free before midnight. 
Saturday
White Material w/ Young Male/ Galcher Lustwerk/ Morgan Louis // Start off your evening at Halcyon the Shop, tonight hosting an extra special White Material showcase! Young Male, Galcher Lustwerk, and Morgan Louis will bathe you in atmospheric, genre-defying sounds.
Electric Minds 10: Sublimate with Ben UFO and Joy Orbison // Here from the UK, the prolific Ben UFO headlines this Saturday edition of Sublimate, exploring sounds both old and new, across worlds of house, garage, dubstep, and grime. He's joined by fellow Brit Joy Orbison, whose sets veer from atmospheric and moody into broken beats and bass patterns. These two beacons of UK dance music will take you on a wild ride lasting into AM hours, so pack your sunglasses for that moment when you are finally thrown back onto the sidewalk.
// Heads up that Ben UFO and Joy Orbison will also be headlining MoMA PS1 earlier this afternoon!
Powell / Russell Haswell / Not Waving / Prostitutes // Diagonal North America Tour 2016 // This 5-year anniversary party for Diagonal Records is taking over both floors of Trans-Pecos, bringing a lineup packed with both local and international talent: live sets from Powell, Russell Haswell, Not Waving, and Prostitutes, DJ sets from Bookworms, L. Sangre, Maria Chavez b2b Wall Peeler, and Scallywag b2b R Gamble. This will be a one-of-a-kind expedition through the wilderness of techno, ambient electronics, machinery, and futuristic sounds.
The Carry Nation: Residents Night // The Carry Nation are back for a birthday celebration and they're bringing their friends! Expect a vibrant selection of dance music and a queer-leaning crowd.
PARTY A Pleasure W00dy Stress Staczie pink0 PARTY // Sweat Equity brings us another lineup of eclectic and genre-itinerant rising talent: A Pleasure, W00dy, Stress, STACZIE, and pink0.
Sunday
TIKI DISCO AT The Well August 21st // Not techno, and not necessarily queer (unless you find us in the crowd, usually near the front left speaker). Soak up these sultry summertime house vibes and let your weekend slowly melt away. Tunes spun by Eli Escobar, Lloydski, and Andy Pry.
Pearson Sound, True Nature & Francois K. // Even more cutting-edge UK dance music to round out your weekend, this time from Hessle Audio's Pearson Sound. He's joined by True Nature, whose sets explore the spaces between genres, as well as the legendary François K.
