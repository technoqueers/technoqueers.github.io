The Legacy of Aaron-Carl // From raunchy ghettotech to haunting house, the artist known as Aaron-Carl was instrumental in Detroit electronic music of the 1990s and 2000s. Marke B. explores his legacy as an outspoken gay man making music in a scene not known for its candor.
Thursday
No Entry w Bobby Browser @ 1271 Myrtle Ave // 10 PM - 4 AM // Dance in an inclusive space for celebrating the origins of techno. Bobby Browser (100% Silk) is joined by Night Doll & DJ Wawa. Free
Dissolution: Alex Alben / Rachel Noon / AMD / Xiorro @ The Gateway // 10 PM - 4 AM // Harder techno from Alex Alben, Rachel Noon, A.M.D., & Xiorro, taking over the 1st floor. Free
MOTOR x Ethereal Beatbox presents: IVVY @ El Cortez // 10-4 AM // Live techno! Seattle-based artist IVVY will be serving up a hardware set. They're joined by Richard P, M Parent, Circe, & Patient. Free
Friday
Lakker [Live] - Tundra A/V, Gunnar Haslam, Aquarian @ Sunnyvale // midnight - 4 AM // A rare chance to catch Lakker, playing a live A/V set inspired by their Tundra LP. Intricate sonic tapestries timed with visuals. They're joined by Gunnar Haslam & Aquarian. Tix
THE BUTTONS X UNTER COLLECTION @ Who can say?? // 2 AM - ?? // The creators of the infamous Berlin-based party Homopatik (RIP) have in its place created Buttons. Madalba & Jacob Meehan (also of Men's Room) are joined by Volvox, Mary Yuzovskaya, & Victoria Mussi. Ambient Room: R Gamble, Turtle Bugg, Nick Bazzano, & Kiddo Sincere.
Saturday
Kiss & Tell + Lost Soul Enterprises present DJ Minx, Simonetti @ Knockdown Center // 2-9 PM // DJ Minx runs Women on Wax and is one of Detroit's best. She will make you shake your ass to house music beneath the sunshine, joined by Mike Simonetti & Scott Murakami. Enjoy the lush garden atmosphere, plus cocktails & food served. Tix
Nights of the Templar @ The Dreamhouse aka The Spectrum // 10 PM - 4 AM // Sets from Nark (Bottom Forty, 2MR), JT Almon (BUTCHED, BROMO), gio black peter, Anthony Dicap, & more. Door: $10 b4 1 AM / $15 b4 3 AM / $20 after. All donations go toward sustaining this queer community space.
Tunnel Vision feat Adam X @ Bossa // 10 PM - 4 AM // An epic 4 hour set from Adam X, NY techno pioneer, founder of Sonic Groove, & co-organizer of the Storm Raves alongside his brother Frankie Bones. Tunnel Vision residents open the evening. Free b4 midnight
