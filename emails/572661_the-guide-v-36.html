NYE is next weekend! Among the parties so far announced, here are the two we're PrEPing for:
Saturday, 12/31
The Spectrum presents DAGGER X OVA @ The Dreamhouse, 1022 Wyckoff Ave. // 10 PM - ??? // Featuring Donatella (A Village Raid), Amber Valentine, Lauren Flax, Octo Octa, and Ne/Re/A, who will be treating us to some hard fucking techno. Door tickets only, $5 off cover if you show up with a well-executed lqqk (theme: rosé/pink champagne, rose gold, chrome pink).
NYE Get Wrecked & Carry @ Analog BKNY // 12 AM - 12 PM // Swarms of shirtless gay boys transform Analog into a space as filthy and sinful as 2016 itself. Wrecked take the decks til 6 AM, and then The Carry Nation pilot this mess til noon. Pounce on advance tickets to avoid waiting on line in the dead of winter in Gowanus. Free, nonconsensual contact high from poppers upon entry.
Thursday
All I want for Christmas is ACID. with Mike Servito & Octo Octa @ Good Room // 10 PM - 4 AM // An acid-drenched holiday special feature (there will apparently be acid xmas deco and reinforced bass). Extended sets served to us by Mike Servito & Octo Octa, amidst the xmas trees and 303's.
Friday
Output Grayscale | Headless Horseman (Live)/ Ambivalent/ DeepChord/ Dadub/ Aris Kindt at Output and Space Is The Place in The Panther Room @ Output // 8 PM - 6 AM // Go with friends and carve out your own safer space on the dance floor (because it's Output), and then seriously lose your mind to Headless Horseman (live), Ambivalent, DeepChord (live), Dadub, Gunnar Haslam, and Aris Kindt. Wander into Panther Room for house sounds from Fred P, Jacky Sommer, and Donny Burlin.
T E CH N O FE M I N IS M @ Bossa // 10 PM - 4 AM // Avoid Output's cover and Friday night clientele and get your mind bent on a more liberated dance floor, courtesy of Mor Elian (performing an extended set!) and Beta Librae.
High+TIGHT x Ruffhouse : As ABOVE so BELOW 12/23 @ The Gateway // 11 PM - 5 AM // Gaaay!!! High+Tight & Ruffhouse join forces, bringing house and disco heaven to the main room, and transforming the basement into "gay hell". Tunes from DatKat, Will Automagic, and SPRKLBB. Obviously clothing optional, complimentary coat check all night.
Saturday
 
Xmas eve with Juliana Huxtable, Rose E Kross, Bearcat @ Bossa // 10 PM - 4 AM // Juliana Huxtable, Rose E Kross, and Bearcat invite us disrupt Xmas eve's holiness and silence. Queer dance floor family is here for you on this holiday evening.
Sunday
BOSSA XMAS @ Bossa // 10 PM - 4 AM // Maybe your employer will give you Monday off, allowing you to dance til extra late, since Xmas lands on a Sunday this year? Or maybe not, since this is America. Secret Santa Miguel Senquiz spins alongside Bad Elves Beto Cravioto and Keith Edward.
Wednesday, 12/28
pure immanence v @ Bossa // 7 PM - 4 AM // An extra-long holiday edition of pure immanence featuring extra-long sets from Auspex, Open Tabs, Kiddo Sincere b2b Nick Bazzano, and Shawn O'Sullivan.
