Refusing to dance in secret: The Black Madonna on Trump

Winter in America by Jack Halberstam

We must resist this regime and express our queerness more powerfully than ever before. We must channel our anger, fear, and despair into action. And we must take care of ourselves and each other.

Join us tomorrow night as we reject our country's new fascist leader, his followers, and everything they stand for. From the bottom of our hearts, our rave is a massive Fuck You to these people, just as the acid house movement was a giant Fuck You to Margaret Thatcher and her ilk. Our rave will be in a clothing-optional warehouse in Bushwick, with performance art, immersive techno, and a maze for cruising made of vinyl.

(( mix of the week ))
(( FJAAK ))

FJAAK is a young trio born and living in Berlin who produces futuristic techno with a nostalgic flavor. They write music incorporating production tools both digital and analog, and they're celebrated for delivering legendary hardware-only live sets. Friends since childhood, these three also carry the distinction of being the youngest live act to have ever been booked at Berghain. During these live sets, all three of their inputs are fused together, creating a unique sound. Consider them one unified being comprised of three humans: they share a home, spending all day living and breathing music together, and they also date each other (just kidding! But we wish we weren't because that would be super hot).

Their NY debut is tomorrow night at our rave, which we think is pretty fucking special. See you on the dancefloor!
