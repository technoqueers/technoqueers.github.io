(( MIX OF THE WEEK ))
(( Rrose ))
TRACKS: Waterfall // Purge

Rrose crafts hypnotic, driving techno, infused with industrial, ambient, and noise influences. A musical alias of Seth Horvitz, Rrose has released on Sandwell District, and runs label Eaux for his own productions. The Rrose moniker references Rrose Sélavy, who is Horvitz’s visual influence. When performing as this queer side of himself, he hopes to inspire queerness in others. Rrose's stage presence is also meant as a playful protest against male-dominated dance music culture. Catch Rrose this Saturday!
Thursday
Blanc presents: Volvox / Mary Yuzovskaya / OG AG // Start your 4-day weekend dancing to our underground favorites, but at Cielo instead of in the environment otherwise indicated by the event flyer. Volvox and Mary Yuzovskaya will be joined by DC10 resident OG AG. Cielo boasts a Funktion-One and a cozy outdoor smoking area!

Capriccio w/ Silent Servant◆Xeno & Oaklander◆Stallone the Reducer◆Alex from Queens◆Analog Soul◆Jake Reif◆SSPS // An extraordinary evening of techno! TQ REDUCED LIST ALERT! No Place Like . . // Just say "Techno Queers" at the door for $10 off cover! House of Yes, our favorite above-ground, bejeweled temple featuring dancing cages and award-winning bathrooms, hosts an exciting house-leaning lineup: Troy Pierce, Heatthrob (live), David Hohme, Tasha Blank, Seth Hosko, Nordvolk, and Kittypep.

Saturday
★ ★ ★ Fourth World 2K16 ★ ★ ★ // This insane roster of local independent artists is certainly the best way to celebrate Independence Day this weekend. Designed to make you stay for the full 18 hour stretch, this event boasts such amenities as soil, foliage, legit catered food, an abundance of clean toilets, and high speed internet.
Warm Up 7/2: Theo Parrish / DJ STINGRAY / Lena Willikens / Kiki Kudo // MoMA PS1 hosts your daytime solution to missing the nighttime sets of these genius selectors: Theo Parrish, DJ Stingray, Lena Willikens, and Kiki Kudo.
Chromat X Discwoman Pool Party // Discwoman and Chromat join forces to bring you poolside techno bliss, accented by structural experiments for the human body. Artists: Volvox, Umfang, Olive T.
