(( mix of the week ))
(( The Carry Nation ))

Our mix of the week is from The Carry Nation, comprised of Will Automagic & Nita Aviance. They've been producing tracks and remixes for the past 5 years for such labels as Batty Bass, TRAX, Nervous, Get Up, TRIBAL, Night People, DFA, & Twirl. Their party, also named The Carry Nation, started at Brooklyn's infamous after-hours spot The Spectrum, and lived at both Steel Drums and 43 Scott before landing at Good Room. Their crowd is a mix of gay music heads and their straight friends who get it... Their productions and the infamy of their parties have brought them all around the world in recent years. But in the end, their home is where their heart is: deep in the city of New York.

This Saturday, The Carry Nation take over Good Room, joined by Volvox, with Bearcat and Mauro Feola holding down the Bad Room! Advance tickets cost less than a cocktail in Manhattan!
Thursday
Purple Trax Label Night Vol XIV @ Bossa // A special night of experimentation in electronic music, this time featuring AAAA (live), a Mexican DJ and producer who gave us a fabulous Boiler Room session. He's joined by Blue Hawaii, Greem Jellyfish (live), and residents Logan Takahashi and Erez Avissar.
Friday
Mysteries of the Deep • Divination @ 315 Ten Eyck St. // A extraordinary curation of ambient electronic acts that will transport you to other worlds: Antenes (live), Mark Verbos (live), Clinker (live av), Abby Echiverri (live av), Patrick Russell, and Grant Aaron.
French Kiss presents Paranoid London // A Guy Called Gerald @ Good Room // Live sets from A Guy Called Gerald, legendary forefather of acid house and drum'n'bass, and Paranoid London, a duo who has sold more of their debut album than anyone else without any press, reviews, or promotional copies given away. Also: do not miss Ital's live set in the Bad Room!
Project Pablo [LIVE], Umfang, Max McFerren @ Trans-Pecos // A rare live set from Montreal-based DJ and musician Project Pablo. He's joined by Umfang and Max McFerren for a 1080p-infused evening of dreamy and cutting edge house music.
Saturday
The Carry Nation w/ Volvox, Bearcat, and Mauro Feola @ Good Room // TQ favorites The Carry Nation and Volvox are joined by Bearcat and Mauro Feola. Loads of techno, acid, and house enjoyed by a queer crowd. See you on the dance floor!
Closed Circuit VIII (Clouds / Broken English Club / Ontal) @ Secret Location // Darker and more experimental shades of techno until 9 AM from special guests Clouds (NY debut), Broken English Club (live), and Ontal, joined by locals L.Sangre and Scallywag b2b R Gamble.
Sunday
pure immanence ii @ Bossa // Assuming you made it home in time to get some sleep prior to this event: gather here and soothe your soul with techno. Sets from locals Peter Fonda, Kiddo Sincere b2b Nick Bazzano, Turtle Bugg, and Olga.
