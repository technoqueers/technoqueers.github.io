(( artist of the week ))
(( Midland ))

In a few short years, Harry Agius, a.k.a. Midland, has quietly become one of dance music’s most beloved producers and DJs. Since emerging in the UK in 2010, Midland has released a stream of singles crisscrossing multiple genres and styles. Though originally labeled as a UK bass producer, he has proven himself adept at creating melodic, air-tight house tracks, techno-leaning club cuts, and most recently, disco-inspired edits. He has released on labels such as Phonica, Aus, Graded (his own label), and JD Twitch of Optimo’s Autonomous Africa -- a  label whose proceeds go toward funding the Mtandika Mission in Tanzania, a charity run by Midland’s parents. In 2015, he launched sub-imprint ReGraded, focused solely on sample-based house and disco, whose releases “Double Feature” and “Final Credits” have soundtracked innumerable peak dancefloor moments in their short lifespan.

This mix, recorded live in Belfast last year as part of Midland’s “All Night Long UK” tour, is a remarkable feat of creativity and endurance. Over the course of five hours, he expertly blends foot-stomping disco, house, soul, African boogie, and throbbing techno in a way that is perfectly paced and utterly captivating. There is a beautiful arc to the set, and as grooves wax and wane, dazzling moments of high energy are offset by moodier, left-field selections that gently re-adjust the mood. As is typical of his DJ sets, the resulting experience is both elegantly understated and raucously fun.

We are incredibly fortunate to have Midland joining us for Wrecked X The Bunker this Saturday at Good Room as part of his eight-stop US tour, where he’ll be sharing the bill with local favorites Mike Servito and Ryan Smith, as well as Giegling’s Leafar Legov and ATEQ. See you there!
