(( MIX OF THE WEEK ))
(( Miguel Senquiz ))

Output resident and Detroit native Miguel Senquiz of Spectral Sound, Ghostly, and Drip brings his energetic blend of house, techno, and acid to Cultivated Sound this Friday.
Thursday
Synthicide | Antoni Maiovvi, Life for Dead b2b Jordan Passmore, Cute Heels, Andi // Fog, lasers, and synth-driven DJ sets from Antoni Maiovvi, Life for Dead b2b Jordan Passmore, Cute Heels, and Andi at Bossa.
Friday
RSVP for REDUCED LIST: Cultivated Sound presents: DJ Qu, Inigo Kennedy & Miguel Senquiz // Join us for this insane house and techno lineup featuring DJ Qu, Inigo Kennedy, Miguel Senquiz, Maŕa and RTMC.
Abstract Conditions: Speak Onion / Solaris / Bastet / C10 / Peter Seligman // Techno-leaning genre misfits take over the basement of Trans-Pecos, this time featuring breakcore artist Speak Onion (live), joined by Bastet (live), Solaris, C10, and Peter Seligman (live).
GROOVY GROOVY pres. LIMIT (L-VIS 1990 + EGYPTRIXX) // Worldwide debut of LIMIT (Night Slugs co-founder L-VIS 1990 & EGYPTRIXX), joined by Stud1nt, Teleself, Akanbi, and Yogic (upstairs at Trans-Pecos).
Saturday
RSVP for REDUCED LIST // TQ TAKEOVER: Analog BKNY presents: Crashbeat with Regis, Veronica Vasicka, Reade Truth, Jake Reif & Sergio Dimoff // How could we NOT take this over? Don't forget to RSVP for this sonic journey led by techno legends, as experienced through Analog's superior sound system: Regis, Veronica Vasicka, Reade Truth, Jake Reif, and Sergio Dimoff.
Tunnel Vision w/ Morgan Louis / Greg Z / Residents // Heady techno from White Material's Morgan Louis, joined by Greg Z and residents at Bossa.
NO EXIT // House, dance, and electro sets in an abandoned and re-purposed bank in Bed-Stuy, featuring Tiger Tooth, Katie Rex, TAUL PAUL / Darth Paul, and Dad Jeans. One time only and no cover!
FIXED x Wrecked + Rory Phillips and Sprklbb in the Bad Room // FIXED (JDH & Dave P) & Wrecked (Ryan Smith & Ron Like Hell) fill Good Room with vibrant house music, joined by Rory Phillips and Sprklbb.
Sunday
Cultivated Sound presents: Rawaat, Zeni Karpuzi & Jules // An exciting mix of house and techno soundscapes awaits you this Sunday at Bossa: Rawaat of Lobster Theremin is joined by Zeni Karpuzi, Jules, and Cultivated sound residents Maŕa and RTMC.
Techno Support Group: Rezzie Avissar/See Other/Lily Ray/Heidi Sabertooth // Rezzie Avissar, See Other, Lily Ray, and Heidi Sabertooth are here to support you with some techno, and also some non-techno if that is what you need at this moment.
