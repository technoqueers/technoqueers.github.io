+ Located at 244 East 53rd Street in midtown Manhattan, Cowboy & Cowgirl was a gay bar & restaurant that operated during the 1970s & early 1980s. It was known for its extended hours (noon to 4 AM daily), disco music, & casual, unpretentious menu featuring “man-sized hamburgers,” Mexican chili, & chicken in a basket. The venue was a fixture in queer nightlife at the time, & was particularly associated with hustler culture. It was also frequented by a mix of regulars & several notable, high-profile celebrities.
