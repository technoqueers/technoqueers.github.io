We're proud to announce that TQ has joined PACBI, the Palestinian Academic and Cultural Boycott of Israel. This means that our newsletter will continue to exclude any content that normalizes the Israeli state.

Club culture plays a central role in Israel’s campaign to market and legitimize itself internationally. PACBI, the cultural arm of Boycott, Divestment, and Sanctions, advocates for a boycott of Israeli academic and cultural institutions for their deep and persistent complicity in Israel’s denial of Palestinian rights.

Dweller, Technomaterialism, and many others have endorsed PACBI.

Writers Against the War on Gaza is eager to support nightlife institutions interested in joining PACBI. If you're involved in a party, venue, record label, radio station, or other platform, we urge you to reach out to pacbi@wawog.com with any questions and to endorse.
