Excited for Amazon’s Arrival in Long Island City? The Results for New York’s Art Community Won’t Be Pretty // New York already suffers from intense inequality-driven pressures of its own. If you prefer your critique in YouTube form, take a look at this parody video of Amazon’s “Real Life in Seattle” corporate recruiting video, which went viral a few years ago.
