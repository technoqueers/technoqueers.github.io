3/18: We're taking over Grayscale

This is the best techno next Saturday. And with all of us taking over the dancefloor, it may also be the queerest. RSVP to join our reduced door list: $20 entry all night, scandalously cheap for this level of talent!
Thursday
Make Techno Black Again HH @ 1271 Myrtle Ave. // 8-10 PM // Make Techno Black Again hats will be on sale at this prequel to Breakz: Neuroshima / Shy Eyez / Kellam Matthews. 20% of profits will go to Real Life. My Music.
Marie Davidson / Veronica Vasicka / Further Reductions + more @ Good Room // 10 PM - 4 AM // Hypnotic rhythms from Marie Davidson (live), joined by Vernoica Vasicka, Further Reductions (live), and DJ Scallywag. As if this weren't enough to keep you out late on a Thursday, Acid Camp takes over the Bad Room: sets from Aaron Davis and Miguel Senquiz. Tix / $20 door
Friday
The Bunker presents Lag, Oliver Hafenbauer & Jane Fitz @ Good Room // 10 PM - 6 AM // Mord's Lag and DJs' DJ Oliver Hafenbauer grace the turntables, alongside Clay Wilson & Oliver Chapoy (btw, check out their Sensoria series, Sundays @ Jupister Disco). Bad Room: Jane Fitz is joined by Sublimate residents Turtle Bugg and Sagotsky. Tix / Door $20? / Free with RSVP via Bunker Drip membership
GROOVY GROOVY — Rune Bagge & Antenes @ RSVP for location // 10 PM - 8 AM // Ectotherm's Rune Bagge descends upon our underground, joining Antenes, who will be throwing down a twisted all-vinyl set. The vibe will be sweaty, ravey, and strictly freaky. Vveiss, Olga, Nk Badtz Maru, Teleself, Akanbi, and Yogic will help conjure this voodoo. This is your all-night plan or your Bunker afterparty. Tix
