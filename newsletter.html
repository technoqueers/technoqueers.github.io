Saturday, 19 Ago  

Femmequerade Ball

This party is taking over Good Room and turning it into a specifically queer/femme-positive space! It’s co-hosted by Brujas, the Bronx-based all-woman skater crew (recently profiled in the NY Times), as well as an inclusive electronic music collective known as Sister, and a satirical “art-bro” duo called Hot Schmucks. Door tickets are $18 for cis straight white men and $15 for everyone else.
