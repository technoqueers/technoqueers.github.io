Mix of the week

Saturday

Pinch, Graphs, Aquarian, True Nature

<a href='https://soundcloud.com/dj-pinch'>Pinch</a> brings an otherworldly mix of deep, dark &amp; broken-beat techno rhythms, seismic UK-dubstep, grime, dancehall, and future-dub. He brings along <a href='https://soundcloud.com/graphs'>Graphs</a>, <a href='https://www.facebook.com/aquarianyes/'>Aquarian</a>, and <a href='https://www.facebook.com/truenature.4thtemple/'>True Nature</a> for a dubby night of exquisite musical selections.

Femmequerade Ball

This party is taking over Good Room and turning it into a specifically queer/femme-positive space! It’s co-hosted by <a href='https://www.facebook.com/wearebrujas/'>Brujas</a>, the Bronx-based all-woman skater crew (<a href='http://www.nytimes.com/2016/07/31/nyregion/brujas-a-crew-of-female-skateboarders-in-the-bronx.html?_r=0'>recently profiled in the NY Times</a>), as well as an<em> </em>inclusive electronic music collective known as <a href='https://thump.vice.com/en_uk/track/sister-collective-founder-toxes-new-mix-is-overwhelming-in-the-best-way'>Sister</a>, and a satirical “art-bro” duo called <a href='https://www.facebook.com/hotschmucks/'>Hot Schmucks</a>. Door tickets are $18 for cis straight white men and $15 for everyone else.

Confused House

A pioneer of the NYC techno scene known for crossing between techno and industrial, <a href='https://www.residentadvisor.net/dj/adamx'>Adam X</a> promises to deliver a hard-hitting set punctuated by ambient and minimal sounds that somehow fall perfectly into place. He's joined by <a href='https://soundcloud.com/antenes'>Antenes</a>, one of our local favorites known for delivering eclectic techno sets that drift into experimental and psychedelic realms.
